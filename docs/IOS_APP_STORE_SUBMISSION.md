## 2. App Description

### Short Description (for Promotional Text, 170 chars max)

Track, score, and analyze your shooting sports sessions. Log every shot, time your series, and export results to Excel. Supports 22+ disciplines in 8 languages.

### Full Description

TriggerTime is the ultimate companion for competitive and recreational shooters. Whether you practice air pistol, centerfire, rapid fire, bench rest, or historical MLAIC disciplines, TriggerTime helps you log every session, score every shot, and track your progress over time.

**Session Logging Made Simple**
Create sessions in seconds. Choose your discipline, set the date, pick your location, and select the session type — from casual training to national championships. TriggerTime adapts to your discipline's specific scoring format, phases, and shot counts.

**Two Scoring Modes**
- **Shot-by-Shot**: Log each individual shot with a fast numpad interface (0-10, X, Miss). Scores and X-counts are calculated automatically.
- **Per-String**: Enter total scores per string for faster data entry. Optionally track First Miss for disciplines that require it.

**Built-in Shot Timer**
Use the integrated shot timer during scoring to pace your series. Configurable wait and fire durations with audio cues (warning beep, fire beep, end beep). Supports continuous beep mode for rapid-fire disciplines.

**22+ Pre-loaded Disciplines**
Covers a wide range of ISSF, national, and historical shooting disciplines:
- Air Pistol (10m), Center-Fire Pistol (25m), Sport Pistol (25m), Standard Pistol, Rapid Fire Pistol, 9mm Pistol, Free Pistol (50m)
- Air Rifle (10m), BR-50, BR-50 Air Rifle, F-Class (50m/100m/Rimfire), Lightweight Sport Rifle (LWSR), Open Sights Rifle
- MLAIC historical disciplines with 22 events and 6 categories
- 15+15 format variants for multiple pistol disciplines

**Custom Disciplines**
Create your own disciplines with custom names, weapon types, scoring methods, phase structures, and shot counts.

**Session Types**
Categorize every session: Training, Social, Provincial Championship, Regional Championship, National Championship, and more. Session types are color-coded for quick identification.

**Sighting Series Support**
Optionally record sighting series before scored phases. Sighting shots are tracked separately and do not affect your scored totals.

**Session History & Review**
Browse all your sessions organized by date. View detailed breakdowns by phase, series, and individual shots. Edit any session's metadata, scores, or notes at any time.

**Export to Excel**
Export one or multiple sessions to a professionally formatted Excel spreadsheet. Filter by date range and session type. Share via email, cloud storage, or any app on your device.

**Dark Mode**
Full light and dark theme support that follows your system preference or can be set manually.

**8 Languages**
Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Catalan, Basque, and Galician.

**Offline-First**
All data is stored locally on your device. TriggerTime works fully offline — no internet connection required for session logging, scoring, or reviewing your history.

## 4. Full Feature List (Free)

### 4.1 Session Management

| Feature | Description |

### 5.2 Shot Timer

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Integrated Shot Timer** | Built-in timer displayed during scoring screens. Configurable per-phase with wait duration and fire duration. |
| **Audio Cues** | Three distinct audio signals: warning beep (preparation), fire beep (start shooting), and end beep (cease fire). |
| **Continuous Beep Mode** | Optional continuous beeping during fire phase for rapid-fire disciplines that require audible pacing. |
| **Pause / Resume** | Pause and resume the timer mid-series without losing progress. |
| **Per-Phase Configuration** | Each phase in a discipline defines its own timer settings (wait seconds, fire seconds, timer type). |

### 6.3 Subscription Feature Justification (for App Review)

**Unlimited Sessions** — The free tier allows users to fully experience all core functionality (scoring, session management, disciplines) with up to 10 sessions. PRO+ removes this limit for active shooters who train regularly and need ongoing session history.

**Shot Timer** — An advanced training tool that integrates audio-timed series into the scoring workflow. Adds real-time pacing with configurable wait/fire durations and audio cues. Not required for basic scoring functionality.

**Excel Export** — Professional data export for users who need to analyze performance, share results with coaches, or submit scores to clubs/federations. Generates formatted XLSX files with complete session data.

**Custom Disciplines** — Power-user feature for shooters in non-standard disciplines or clubs with unique formats. The 22+ pre-loaded disciplines cover all common ISSF and national formats. Custom disciplines serve edge cases and specialized training.

### 6.4 Subscription Terms (for App Store Metadata)

- Payment will be charged to the user's Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.

- Subscriptions may be managed and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to Account Settings after purchase.
- Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription.

## 9. Privacy & Data Collection

### Data Not Collected
TriggerTime stores all session and scoring data locally on the user's device. No personal shooting data is transmitted to external servers.

### Data Collected

| Data Type | Purpose | Linked to User |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| Device ID | License validation and feature unlock | No |
| Device Info (OS, model) | Device registration and support | No |
| License Number | Subscription/license activation | Yes (if provided) |
| Email (via license) | License management | Yes (if provided) |

### Data Not Linked to User
- App usage/analytics: None collected
- Shooting scores/sessions: Stored locally only
- Location data: User-entered text (not GPS), stored locally only
