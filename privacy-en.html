Privacy Policy for TriggerTime

Last updated: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TriggerTime. We value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within our application. The core principle of our app is that we do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with anyone.

2. Information Collection

Our application does not require user account registration. We do not request or collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, phone number, or precise GPS location) for the use of the application.

3. Training and Session Data

The primary function of TriggerTime is to help you record and analyze your sports performance.

  • Data Type: The app allows you to input technical data related to your shooting practice (scores, training dates, ammunition types, equipment notes, etc.).
  • Storage: All information you enter is stored exclusively locally on your device's internal memory.

4. No Data Transfer

Since the application operates entirely offline:

  • We do not send your data to external servers or the "cloud."
  • We (the developer) do not have access to your training data or historical logs.
  • If you delete the application from your device, the stored data will be permanently deleted (unless you have performed a manual backup of the device).

5. Device Permissions

To function correctly, the application may request access to certain native features of your device. These permissions are used strictly for the app's functionality and not to collect data about you:

  • Storage / Files: To save the database of your scores and training sessions on the device.

6. Third-Party Services

This application does not use third-party analytics services or advertising networks that track user behavior. High privacy standards are fundamental to our mission.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at help@triggertime.es.
